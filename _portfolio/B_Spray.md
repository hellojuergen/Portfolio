A masterclass in contrast featuring Severin Guggemoos in Laax. This piece is meticulously divided into four quadrants using an inverse color palette and a striking duotone focus. By isolating the spray in monochromatic black and white, a fundamental freeskiing move is transformed into a spectacular visual. Severin appears in every section, perfectly capturing the delicate balance of the sport.
