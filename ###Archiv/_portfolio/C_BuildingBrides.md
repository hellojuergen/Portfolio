Looking up at Lorenzo Quinn’s 'Building Bridges' from the waterline of the Arsenale. From this low vantage point, the six pairs of monumental hands soar above like a white resin vault. Each arch represents a core value—Friendship, Wisdom, Help, Faith, Hope, and Love—creating a rhythmic canopy against the Venetian sky that feels both grounded and divine.
